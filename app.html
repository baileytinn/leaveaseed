About This Seed

A beloved heirloom tomato originating from the Cherokee people of Tennessee. Produces large, dusky rose-purple fruits with a rich, complex, smoky flavor. A true garden showstopper with unmatched taste.

Seed Specs
Days to Germination7–14 days
Days to Maturity80–90 days
Sun RequirementFull Sun
Germination Rate92%
Certified✓ Non-GMO, Heirloom
How to Grow
1
Start seeds indoors 6–8 weeks before last frost in moist potting mix
2
Keep soil warm at 70–80°F. Seeds germinate in 7–14 days
3
Transplant outdoors after last frost, spacing 24–36 inches apart in full sun
4
Water deeply 1–2 times per week. Stake or cage plants as they grow
5
Harvest when deeply colored and slightly soft. Save seeds from best fruits!
