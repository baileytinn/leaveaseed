✦ Our Process

How Vendor Approval Works

We vet every vendor before they go live. This protects our growers and ensures that being listed on Leave a Seed actually means something.

1

Submit Application

Fill out the form with your company details, seed types, and any certifications you hold.

2

We Review

Our team reviews your application within 5–7 business days. We may reach out with follow-up questions.

3

Approval & Onboarding

Approved vendors receive onboarding instructions and work with our team to set up their listings.

4

Go Live

Your seeds go live with a verified badge and start reaching thousands of growers across America.

Not every application will be approved. We prioritize vendors who grow or source seeds in the United States, hold relevant certifications, and share our commitment to quality and transparency.

✦ What We Look For

Our Vendor Standards

✓

US-Based Operations

We prioritize vendors who grow, source, or operate within the United States to support American agriculture and reduce supply chain uncertainty.

✓

Accurate Germination Rates

We ask vendors to provide honest, tested germination data. Misleading claims are grounds for removal from the platform.

✓

Non-GMO or Clearly Labeled

All seeds must be clearly labeled regarding GMO status. We welcome all seed types but require full transparency in every listing.

✓

Honest Product Descriptions

Listings must accurately describe variety, days to maturity, sun requirements, and growing zones. We review listings before they go live.

✓

Responsive to Buyers

Vendors are expected to respond to buyer questions and handle order issues in a timely manner. Unresolved complaints affect vendor standing.

✓

Mission Alignment

We look for vendors who care about more than volume. A commitment to biodiversity, sustainable growing, and community impact matters to us.

These standards exist to protect growers. We'd rather have fewer, better vendors than a long list of unreliable ones.
