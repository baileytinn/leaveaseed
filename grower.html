✦ What You Get

Everything a Grower Needs

🌱

Curated Seed Marketplace

Browse 12,000+ heirloom, organic, and open-pollinated seed varieties from 500+ vetted American vendors — all in one place with verified reviews from real growers.

📍

Regional Growing Guides

Get personalized planting calendars, frost date alerts, and growing tips matched to your specific ZIP code and climate zone. No more guessing what to plant and when.

📚

Free Education Library

Access beginner to advanced growing guides, video walkthroughs, soil preparation tutorials, pest management advice, and seed-saving techniques — all completely free.

🤝

Community & Forums

Connect with growers in your region. Share harvests, ask questions, swap seeds, and find local community garden opportunities in your neighborhood.

📊

Track Your Impact

Every seed you buy contributes to our hunger mission. Your personal dashboard shows how many meals your purchases have helped enable for food-insecure families.

🎁

Seed Saving Program

Learn to save seeds from your harvest, build your own seed library, and swap with other growers in our community exchange — reducing cost and preserving biodiversity.
