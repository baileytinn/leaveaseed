✦ What We Stand For

Three Pillars.
One Mission.

The Marketplace

A verified, consolidated platform connecting America's best seed companies — heirloom, organic, specialty, and regional — with farmers, gardeners, and growers who need quality they can trust.

  • Every vendor independently vetted and certified
  • Transparent sourcing and germination data
  • Secure payments and buyer protection
  • Reviews from real growers nationwide

The Education

Free, accessible guides, videos, and resources teaching anyone — from apartment dwellers to rural homesteaders — how to grow their own food, no experience required.

  • Step-by-step growing guides for all skill levels
  • Regional planting calendars by US zip code
  • School and community garden programs
  • Expert Q&A and grower community forums

The Mission

Every transaction on Leave a Seed directly funds seed donations, community gardens, and food programs in underserved American communities — turning purchases into purpose.

  • 5% of every sale funds hunger programs
  • Seeds donated to food deserts across the US
  • Partnerships with food banks and co-ops
  • Transparent impact reporting every quarter
