✦ What You'll Need

Starter Tools & Supplies

🪣

Watering Can

A gentle-spout can for precise watering without disturbing seedlings

🌱

Seed Packets

Start with radishes, lettuce, beans, or cherry tomatoes — all forgiving choices

🪴

Pots or Raised Bed

Even a 5-gallon bucket works! Drainage holes are essential

🌍

Potting Mix

Quality potting mix, not garden soil, for containers and raised beds

🌞

Sunlight (6+ hrs)

Most vegetables need full sun — find your sunniest spot

📋

Garden Journal

Track what you plant, when, and what works — it's worth it

🌿 Grower's Tip

Start small. One raised bed or three containers is plenty for your first season. Most beginner overwhelm comes from planting too much at once — focus on a few crops and do them well.
